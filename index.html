Hej, I'm Nele

I’ve always been fascinated by how we can make medical care more intelligent, responsive, and humane through machine learning. Over time, my focus has expanded beyond accuracy alone – toward understanding how we can design AI systems that earn and preserve human trust.

As a researcher and PhD student at DFKI, I contribute to the AnoMed project:

  • blending fundamental research with real medical challenges.
  • aiming to advance healthcare while protecting patient privacy through methods like Differential Privacy.
  • bringing together my core interests in medical AI, signal processing, and privacy-preserving learning.
  • allowing me to explore how we can responsibly integrate AI into medicine for real-world impact.
Please feel free to reach out!
