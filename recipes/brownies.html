Rich Chocolate Brownies

Dense, fudgy, full of dark chocolate.

Ingredients

  • 250 g dark chocolate, chopped
  • 200 g butter (plus a little extra for greasing)
  • 150 g raw cane sugar
  • 120 g white sugar
  • 70 g sugar beet syrup (or molasses/golden syrup)
  • 4 eggs
  • 200 g flour
  • 40 g cocoa powder
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (160°C fan). Grease and line a square baking pan (about 20×20 cm).
  2. Melt chocolate and butter together in a heatproof bowl over simmering water. Stir until smooth and slightly cooled.
  3. Whisk in both sugars and syrup until combined.
  4. Let cool slightly.
  5. Add eggs one at a time, whisking briefly after each.
  6. Sift in flour and cocoa powder, and fold gently.
  7. Pour batter into the prepared pan and smooth the top.
  8. Bake for about 25–30 minutes — the center should still be slightly soft.
  9. Let cool completely before cutting into squares.

Tip: Add chopped nuts, chocolate chunks, or a pinch of espresso powder for extra depth of flavor, take care to slightly underbake.
